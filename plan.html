Plan Your Trip

First-time visitor essentials: getting here, getting around, and key local information.

Getting Here

  • Air travel: Most visitors arrive by air via Taniti’s airport.
  • Cruise ship: A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay one night per week.

Transportation & Getting Around

  • Public buses: Taniti City (5:00 a.m.–11:00 p.m.)
  • Private buses: Serve the rest of the island
  • Taxis: Available in Taniti City
  • Rental cars: Near the airport
  • Bikes: Helmets required by law
  • Walkability: Taniti City and Merriton Landing are easy to explore on foot

Important Visitor Information

Currency & Payments

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar. Many businesses accept major credit cards.

Safety

Violent crime is rare. As tourism increases, petty theft can occur—stay aware.

Laws & Practical Info

Alcohol not served/sold midnight–9 a.m. Power outlets are 120V.

Language

Many younger residents speak English. Rural areas may have limited English.

Health Services

One hospital and several clinics; hospital has multilingual employees.

Holidays

Many national holidays—some attractions and restaurants may be closed.
