Quick Info

  • Currency: U.S. dollar accepted (many places also accept cards)
  • Power outlets: 120V (U.S.-style)
  • Safety: violent crime is rare; watch for petty theft
  • Alcohol: no sales midnight–9:00 a.m.
  • Holidays: some attractions may close
